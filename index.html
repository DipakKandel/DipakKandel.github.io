About Me

I am Dipak Kandel, the son of Mr. Mahendra Kandel, a scholarly man with a master's degree in English Literature, a Goodwill Ambassador and recurrent contributor to society. Since winning the national championship, I represented NEPAL to the world in the USA. I've become one of the most iconic figures of Islington College, known for my high-energy approach and dedication to making a difference in the field of technology and political involvement.

I'm motivated to serve society and country through self-involvement or political involvement to create positive changes in society, citizens, and the Nation.
